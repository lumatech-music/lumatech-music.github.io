BODHI ADITYO

Bodhi Adityo is a Belgian DJ, producer, and label archivist with nearly four decades deeply rooted in the underground electronic music scene. Born in 1972 and stepping behind the decks in 1989, Bodhi has spent more than 40 years navigating the evolution of club culture — from the raw warehouse sounds of the early underground to the refined melodic textures of modern techno.

A seasoned selector with a natural instinct for tension and release, Bodhi's musical palette explores the deeper and more atmospheric corners of electronic music. His sound moves between hypnotic Deep House, emotional Melodic House & Techno, powerful Peak-Time Techno, and the raw acidic textures of Acid Techno. Both his DJ sets and productions are known for their evolving late-night storytelling, building low-end pressure before opening into expansive melodic peaks.

In January 2026, Bodhi returned with The Way Home, a powerful melodic techno release on White Noir that highlights his signature sound: driving rhythms layered with progressive and atmospheric melodies designed for the late hours of the dancefloor. The track follows his 2025 downtempo project Static Chills and the mix compilation Horus Club, Vol. 7, demonstrating his versatility across different tempos and moods.

Beyond performing and producing, Bodhi is also active as a label manager, curating music that respects techno’s underground roots while embracing its melodic evolution. Whether crafting peak-time club tracks or immersive listening experiences, his work reflects a lifelong dedication to the transformative power of electronic music.
